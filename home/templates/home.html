About ELDOPOLY WELFARE

Your Trusted Welfare Program for Financial Growth

ELDOPOLY WELFARE is a member-driven welfare program committed to helping our members achieve financial security, access emergency assistance, and invest in community development. Founded in 2010, we have grown to serve thousands of members across Uasin Gishu County and beyond.
